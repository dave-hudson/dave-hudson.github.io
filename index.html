Welcome to my (Dave Hudson’s) space on the Internet.

I collect things I find important under “Elements".

If something gets my attention, and I have time, I like to write Blog posts. When I started the site I wrote a lot about Bitcoin mining, hence the site name. I’m still very much active in the world of distributed ledgers and blockchain, but am a less active observer of cryptocurrencies now.

A long time ago I discovered I needed to keep track of things I’m doing. My latest experiement in this is the “Journal". The journal is just somewhere for me to think aloud - it’s definitely not definitive!
